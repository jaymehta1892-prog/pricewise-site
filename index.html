How price matching works

Which GTA grocery stores actually price match?

Many grocery stores will match a competitor's flyer price if you show it at the till. Done right, you get the sale price without hopping between five stores. Pricewise does the tedious part — reading the flyers and figuring out which of your items are worth matching this week.

Try it free →

A few things worth knowing

  • ✓No Frills, FreshCo and Giant Tiger have price-match policies — each with its own rules.
  • ✕Walmart Canada ended its Ad Match program in 2020, so it isn't a match option anymore.
  • iMetro and Food Basics don't price-match themselves — but their flyers can be used as proof of a lower price at stores that do.

Policies change and a match is never guaranteed — the store always makes the final call. Pricewise shows you the opportunities; the savings reflect personal experience, not a promised outcome.
